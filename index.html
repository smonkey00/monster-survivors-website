About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you battle against waves of monsters that grow increasingly difficult as time passes. Your objective is simple: survive as long as possible while defeating as many monsters as you can.

With each level up, you'll gain access to new abilities and weapons to help you survive the night. Collect experience orbs from defeated enemies to level up and become stronger. How long can you last before the monsters overwhelm you?

Game Features

  • • Endless waves of diverse monsters
  • • Unique character abilities and weapons
  • • Leveling system with ability upgrades
  • • Dynamic difficulty scaling
  • • Engaging pixel art graphics
  • • Immersive sound effects and music

Gameplay

In Monster Survivors, you control a character in a top-down view. The gameplay revolves around a simple yet addictive loop:

Survive

Dodge monsters and avoid getting hit. Your health is limited, so every hit counts!

Attack

Use your weapons and abilities to defeat monsters. Some attacks are automatic while others require activation.

Evolve

Collect experience to level up and choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones to become stronger.

The game continues until you are defeated. Your goal is to survive as long as possible and achieve the highest score you can.

Controls

PC Controls

  • WASD - Move character
  • Space - Activate special ability
  • Tab - View stats and upgrades
  • ESC - Pause game

Mobile Controls

  • Virtual Joystick - Move character
  • Button - Activate special ability
  • Menu Button - View stats and pause game

Tips & Strategies

Beginner Tips

  • • Focus on movement and dodging first
  • • Collect experience orbs whenever possible
  • • Prioritize health upgrades early on
  • • Don't get cornered by monster groups

Advanced Strategies

  • • Create synergies between different abilities
  • • Kite larger monsters around the map
  • • Use terrain and obstacles to your advantage
  • • Balance offensive and defensive upgrades
